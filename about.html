Who We Are

Travel Takes a Toll.
We Handle the Recovery.

Travel can be exciting - but it can also leave you feeling drained. Long flights, busy schedules, hours on your feet, and exploring new cities all take a toll on the body. Rekova was created to bring recovery directly to hotel guests in the privacy and comfort of their own room.

Founded by Tyler Therrien after years in the recovery and wellness industry, Rekova was built around a simple idea: the demand for premium recovery while traveling existed everywhere - but the accessibility didn't.

Until now.
