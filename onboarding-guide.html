Phase 1 — Agreement

Contract Executed

What Happens at Signing
  • ✓Partnership agreement fully executed via DocuSign
  • ✓Billing activated per your selected model — Retainer ($799/mo) or Revenue Share
  • ✓Official Rekova welcome email sent with onboarding timeline and next steps
  • ✓Dedicated account rep assigned to your property — your direct line from here forward
  • ✓Partner communication channel opened (email + text, same-day response SLA)
Your Agreement Covers
  • Program model — Retainer or Revenue Share, clearly defined
  • Term length and renewal terms
  • Full scope of services — equipment, training, support, fly-out included
  • Pricing rights — your hotel sets nightly kit rate (recommended $79–$129/night)
  • Equipment use rights — all devices remain on property for contract duration
Day 1 Deliverable

Signed agreement copy, billing confirmation, full onboarding timeline, and account rep contact info — all delivered within 24 hours of signature.

Phase 3 — Virtual Kickoff

Onboarding Meeting

This virtual kickoff is the strategic heart of your onboarding. The Rekova team walks your property leadership through everything — pricing, selling, guest experience, and what to expect on launch day — before anyone sets foot on-property.

Who Should Attend
  • ✓Hotel General Manager or Operations Director
  • ✓Designated Point of Contact (your Rekova liaison)
  • ✓Front Desk Leads — those who will pitch and present kits to guests
  • ✓Concierge or Guest Services team if applicable at your property
Meeting Logistics
  • Duration: 60–75 minutes via Zoom or Google Meet
  • Scheduled: Within 1–2 weeks of kit delivery confirmation
  • Rekova Team: Account rep + senior partner or founder
  • Recording: Full session recorded and shared within 24 hours

REKOVA RECOVERY Phase 4 · In-Person Visit
06
Phase 4 — On-Site Visit

Rekova Flies Out to Your Property

This is where the partnership goes from virtual to real. A Rekova representative flies directly to your hotel to meet management in person, walk the property, finalize equipment placement, and set the stage for launch day.

What the Visit Covers
1
Meet Hotel Leadership
In-person introduction with GM, management, and key guest-facing staff
2
Full Property Walk-Through
Review room layout, storage options, signage placement, and kit flow logistics
3
Equipment Placement Review
Confirm best location for Cold Plunge setup and kit storage at front desk or bell
4
Rekova Branding & Signage Setup
Branded materials positioned throughout the property to drive guest awareness
5
Management Strategy Session
Revenue targets, VIP guest positioning, upsell integration into check-in flow
Visit Logistics
  • Timing: Typically 2–3 weeks after the onboarding call
  • Duration: Full day on-site (approximately 6–8 hours)
  • Rekova covers: All travel, accommodations, and on-site materials
  • Hotel provides: Space for demo, access to all relevant staff
  • Date confirmed: Locked in during the onboarding call (Step 05)
Day-of Schedule
  • 9:00 AM — Rekova arrival · full property walk-through
  • 10:30 AM — Management strategy & revenue session
  • 12:00 PM — Lunch break · equipment staged for afternoon demo
  • 1:00 PM — Live staff demo begins (all devices — see Step 07)
  • 3:30 PM — Q&A session · go-live checklist review · wrap
What to Prepare Before the Visit

Have all equipment unboxed and staged in one room · Ensure GM or senior management is present for the morning session · Notify staff so maximum team attendance at the afternoon demo

REKOVA RECOVERY Phase 4 · Demo Day
07
Phase 4 — Demo Day

Live Staff Demo & Training

The Rekova team conducts a hands-on, live demonstration of every device with your entire guest-facing staff — exactly how each device works, how the kit is packaged for guests, and how to confidently answer any question a guest may have.

Equipment Demonstrated Live
  • ✓Normatec Go Boots — setup, correct fit, session length, contraindications
  • ✓Red Light Therapy Panel — positioning, session timing, eye protection, key benefits
  • ✓Hyperice Hypervolt — attachment heads, muscle groups, proper pressure technique
  • ✓TheraFace Mask — LED modes, application time, skin care context for guests
  • ✓Depuffing Wand — EMS settings, facial technique, how to demo for guests
  • ✓Therabody ThermBack LED — setup, treatment settings, proper use and sanitation
  • ✓Cold Plunge Tub — fill process, temperature setup, full safety protocol
Kit Packaging Walkthrough
  • Kit assembly: How all devices are packed into the branded Rekova bag for guest delivery
  • Kit configurations: Which items ship together and available add-on options
  • Sanitation protocol: Full cleaning procedure required between every single guest use
  • Storage standard: Exactly how equipment is stored and organized between rentals
  • Guest handoff script: Word-for-word how staff presents the kit at check-in or delivery
Role-Play Scenarios
  • Proactive check-in pitch for athletes, couples, and wellness travelers
  • Answering guest questions about device safety, hygiene, and health benefits
  • Upgrading from base kit to the full recovery suite
  • Handling a device question or issue calmly and professionally
After Demo Day

Every attending staff member receives a printed quick-reference card. Your POC gets a full demo recap document from Rekova within 48 hours. You are cleared for launch.

Phase 5 — Launch

You're Live

Your hotel is now officially a Rekova Recovery partner property. Guests can book premium recovery experiences and your team is fully trained and equipped to deliver them at a luxury standard.

Go-Live Checklist
All equipment staged and ready
Devices sanitized, charged, and packed into kit bags at front desk or bell storage
Entire guest-facing team trained
All staff attended demo, reviewed Sales Guide, and can pitch kits confidently
Nightly pricing confirmed
Kit rate entered in your PMS · add-ons priced and communicated to all staff
Rekova featured in amenities
Kit listed in room collateral, concierge menu, or digital guest guide
POC active and reachable
Point of contact available to field guest inquiries and report first-week data to Rekova
Account rep notified
Confirm go-live date with your rep to activate performance tracking and reporting
Your First 30 Days
  • Week 1: Soft launch — staff actively pitching, tracking every kit request and rental
  • Week 2: Check-in call with Rekova rep — review first bookings, adjust strategy
  • Weeks 3–4: Full push — kit featured in check-in flow and digital amenities
  • Day 30: First official performance report delivered by Rekova
Revenue Benchmarks to Track
  • Break-even point: ~11 kit nights rented per month at $79/night
  • Profit starts: Every rental beyond 11/month is pure revenue above cost
  • Month 1 target: 15–20 kit nights rented
  • Month 3 target: 30+ kit nights = $2,370+/mo above program cost
Rekova's Launch Commitment

Your account rep is on-call for the first two weeks post-launch. Any guest issue, device question, or sales challenge — we respond same day, guaranteed.
