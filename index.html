Our Story

Built by Athletes, For Everyone

Rekova Recovery was born from a simple frustration: world-class recovery tools were only accessible to professional athletes with big budgets and dedicated sports science teams.

We set out to change that. Our mission is to bring elite-level recovery directly to your doorstep — whether you're a weekend warrior, a serious competitor, or someone healing from an injury.
