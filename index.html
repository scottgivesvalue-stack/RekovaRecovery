How It Works

Recovery Delivered
Straight to Your Door

Rekova packages everything into one premium Recovery Kit and delivers it directly to your hotel room - just like room service.

Inside, you'll find professional-grade recovery technology including compression therapy, red light therapy, percussion recovery tools, and curated wellness amenities designed to help you recharge during your stay.

Once you're finished, simply leave the kit in your room and our team will handle the rest.

Every piece of equipment is thoroughly cleaned and sanitized before and after every guest use - so you can focus entirely on recovery, relaxation, and performance.
