How to Sell
Sales Playbook

Target. Pitch.
Close.

Who to Target

  • Athletes & Sports Teams — marathon runners, triathletes, tournament players, coaches. These guests know the products and will be excited.
  • Corporate Travelers — frequent flyers dealing with jet lag, fatigue, and stress. The ThermBack LED and red light alone sell themselves.
  • Wedding Parties & Event Guests — long days, late nights, tired faces. The depuffing wand and facial mask are perfect.
  • Fitness Professionals — personal trainers, yoga instructors, gym owners staying in town for events or certs.
  • Luxury Leisure Travelers — guests who want premium experiences and are already paying for upgrades.

When to Pitch

  • At Check-In — most effective moment. Ask about their trip purpose. "Are you here for an event or training?" opens the door naturally.
  • At Booking Confirmation — include Rekova as an optional add-on in confirmation emails or upsell calls.
  • After the Gym — if you see a guest heading to/from the fitness center, that's your moment.
  • Concierge Desk — display the room card and kit information prominently at concierge/front desk.
  • Multi-Night Stays — guests staying 3+ nights are prime targets. The $79 nightly rate feels low relative to their extended stay.

The Pitch — What to Say

"Good evening — are you here for an event or training this week?" [Pause for answer.]

"Perfect — we actually have something that might be exactly what you need." We offer the Rekova Recovery Kit — it's a full in-room recovery suite with professional-grade compression boots, red light therapy, a percussion gun, LED face mask, a depuffing wand, and a science-backed back relief wrap. It's the same gear used by NFL and Olympic athletes, delivered right to your room.

"It's $79 for tonight — and honestly, guests tell us it's the best sleep they've had in months." Would you like me to have it set up before you get back to your room?

Key Message: You're not selling a gadget — you're selling recovery, results, and a better stay. Guests don't need to know what photobiomodulation means. They need to know: "You'll wake up feeling better tomorrow."

Objection Handling
Handle Every No

Objections &
Answers.

I don't need all that stuff.
That's totally fine — you can use whatever you want. Most guests end up using the percussion gun and ThermBack LED every night. Even one device makes the stay better. It's $79 for all six tools, not just one.
Is it clean / sanitary?
Absolutely. Every kit is fully sanitized and inspected between every use. We follow a strict cleaning protocol — same standards used by professional sports teams.
I don't know how to use any of this.
No experience needed. Every device is intuitive, and each kit includes an instruction card. The compression boots? You just sit back and press one button. We'll walk you through anything you need.
$79 seems like a lot.
Consider what you're getting: six professional recovery devices that would cost $3,000+ to buy. Gyms charge $60–$120 for a single compression session. You're getting a full suite in your room for one night.
I'm only here for one night.
That's actually the perfect time. Red-eye tonight? Compression boots and the ThermBack LED will have you recovered and sharp tomorrow. One night is all you need to feel the difference.
I'll just use the hotel gym.
The gym is great for working out — this is for recovering from working out. They're complementary. Most of our guests use the kit after the gym. Better sleep, faster recovery, better results tomorrow.
✓   DO
✓Ask about their trip purpose before pitching — personalize the pitch
✓Mention specific products that fit their situation (athlete → boots, traveler → ThermBack LED)
✓Emphasize ease of use and in-room convenience
✓Offer to set the kit up before they return to the room
✗   DON'T
✗Don't use technical terms like "photobiomodulation" — say "red light therapy that helps your muscles recover"
✗Don't pitch every guest the same way — read the room
✗Don't push too hard — one follow-up is enough if they decline
✗Don't forget to mention the surprise local gift that's included
